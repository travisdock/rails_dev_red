Debug Console Reference

Open your browser devtools and call these on the window.DEBUG object while the game is running. Useful for skipping grinds, testing battles, or inspecting state. Not available in production builds if stripped.

Party & Gems

DEBUG.healParty()
Fully heals all gems in your party (HP restored to max).
DEBUG.healParty()
DEBUG.addGem(gemId, level?)
Adds a new gem instance to your party. Fails silently if party is full (max 6).
gemId — id of the gem (see list below). level — optional, defaults to 10.
DEBUG.addGem('rspec')
DEBUG.addGem('herb', 25)
DEBUG.setLevel(index, level)
Sets the level of a gem in your party by its slot index. Recalculates max HP and stats, and refills HP.
index — party slot (0-5). level — new level.
DEBUG.setLevel(0, 30)
DEBUG.listGems()
Logs a table of all party gems: name, level, HP, type, and moves.
DEBUG.listGems()

Progress

DEBUG.addBadge(badgeId)
Grants a gym badge. Useful for testing post-gym progression.
badgeId — one of the badge ids below.
DEBUG.addBadge('test_pass')
DEBUG.gameState()
Logs current badges, trainers defeated, and party summary.
DEBUG.gameState()

Save

DEBUG.deleteSave()
Deletes the active save slot. Reload the page to start fresh from the title screen.
DEBUG.deleteSave()
